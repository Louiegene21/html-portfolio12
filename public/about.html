A goal-oriented person is someone who is driven by a clear sense of purpose and direction in their life. They set specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals for themselves and work diligently towards achieving them. These individuals are focused, determined, and motivated to overcome obstacles and challenges in pursuit of their objectives.

"A new person" can also describe someone who has undergone significant changes in their personality, behavior, or outlook on life. It could be a person who has made positive changes, such as adopting healthier habits, improving their attitude, or overcoming personal challenges, resulting in a noticeable transformation.
